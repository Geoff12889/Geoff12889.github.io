About Me

I believe the strongest digital experiences come from the intersection of creativity, technology, and purposeful communication. My career has taken me through military leadership, multimedia production, and now software development. Each of these chapters has shaped how I approach problem-solving and design.

This page offers a deeper look at my professional journey, the experiences that have shaped my perspective, and the values that continue to guide the work I create.

Bio

I'm Geoffrey Woytt, a multimedia specialist, creative professional, and aspiring full-stack web developer with a passion for building meaningful digital experiences.

My professional journey began in the United States Air Force, where I spent over a decade serving in roles that demanded leadership, precision, adaptability, and critical thinking. Throughout my military career, I had the opportunity to lead teams, support mission-critical operations, and develop a strong foundation in problem-solving under pressure.

After transitioning from military service, I continued building on that foundation through multimedia production and strategic communications. My work has included photography, videography, motion graphics, graphic design, branding, and digital content creation. Whether capturing a story through a camera lens or designing visual content for organizational communication, I've always been driven by the challenge of turning ideas into impactful experiences.

Today, I'm expanding that creative foundation into software development. I'm currently pursuing a Bachelor of Science in Graphic Information Technology - Full-Stack Web Development at Arizona State University, where I'm developing technical skills in front-end and back-end development, database design, and user-centered digital experiences.

I enjoy building projects that combine design and functionality. I believe websites should not only work well, but also communicate clearly, feel intuitive, and leave a lasting impression.
